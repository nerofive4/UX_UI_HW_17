I'm Steve, and in 2023, I began my transition from a 14-year career as a communication specialist for an international government contractor to a career as a UX designer. I pursued this change to focus on my passion for exploring new ideas, solving problems, and understanding user needs in order to develop powerful solutions that simplify complex tasks. I take pride in creating user experiences that are both visually appealing and easy to use. My problem-solving approach focuses on understanding the user's desired end state, their needs, and their perception of the process. My background as a communication manager gives me a unique perspective on feature design, user experience, and attention to detail. This allows me to build powerful systems that empower users and help them achieve their goals.

My UX Skills

Communication:

Clearly and concisely explaining your ideas to others.

Problem Solving:

Identifying and solving user problems in a creative and effective way.

Prototyping:

Creating low-fidelity models of your designs to test them with users and get feedback.

User Empathy:

Understanding and advocating for the needs and goals of the end-users throughout the design process.

Analysis:

Analyzing user data and metrics to identify areas for improvement and inform design decisions.

Teamwork:

Collaborating effectively with others to achieve a common goal.
